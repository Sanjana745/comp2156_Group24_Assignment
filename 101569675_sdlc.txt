DevOps plays an important role in the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC).
It connects development and operations teams.
This improves communication and reduces deployment errors.

In traditional SDLC, development and operations worked separately.
DevOps integrates automation tools to speed up the process.
Continuous integration and continuous deployment are key concepts.

DevOps ensures faster testing and feedback.
It improves software quality and stability.
Learning DevOps helps students prepare for modern IT industry roles.

SDLC stands for Software Development Life Cycle.
It includes planning, designing, testing, and deployment.
It ensures software quality and structure.
