George Brown College - short intro
George Brown College is located in Toronto, Canada.
It offers diploma, degree, and postgraduate programs.
The college focuses on practical and hands-on learning.
It has multiple campuses including St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront.
Many international students study here every year.
George Brown also offers industry partnerships.
Students get practical real-world experience.
The college supports career development programs.
George Brown provides career-focused education.
