George Brown College is a well-known public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
It was established in 1967 and has built a strong reputation for providing career-focused education.
The college offers diploma, advanced diploma, degree, and postgraduate certificate programs.

The college has three main campuses: St. James Campus, Casa Loma Campus, and Waterfront Campus.
Each campus is equipped with modern labs, classrooms, and technology to support student learning.
The Waterfront campus specializes in health sciences programs.
